Key Characteristics

Power Output: It provides a prime power output of 30 kVA (24 kW) and a standby power of around 33 kVA (26.4 kW).
Engine: Powered by a 3-cylinder or 4-cylinder, 1500 rpm (for 50 Hz systems) or 1800 rpm (for 60 Hz systems), heavy-duty industrial Perkins diesel engine, known for durability and efficiency.
Operation: Modern units are typically soundproofed with an acoustic canopy, designed for low noise operation (around 61-69 dBA at 7 meters), making them suitable for noise-sensitive environments like residential or commercial areas.
Fuel Efficiency:The diesel engines are highly fuel-efficient, consuming approximately 5.4-7.1 liters per hour at 75% load, which allows for extended runtime with an integral base fuel tank (typically 100L or more).
Features: Standard features often include electric start, a digital control panel for monitoring performance, and safety features like overload and low-oil pressure protection. Many models are also available with an Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) for seamless backup power during outages.
Applications: Its versatility makes it ideal for powering essential equipment in construction, agriculture, small factories, offices, and homes.
These generators are known for their reliable performance and a widespread global support network for parts and service.
